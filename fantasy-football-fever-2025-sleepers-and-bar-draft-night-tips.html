Fantasy Football Fever: 2025 Sleepers & Bar Draft Night Tips”

Fantasy football is a religion in many parts of Canada, and as draft season heats up, savvy managers are looking for sleepers and strategy spots. Here's how to crush your league this year.

Sleeper Picks to Watch

These aren’t household names—yet.

  • Tyjae Spears (RB – TEN): He flashed big-play ability in 2024 and could take over the backfield full-time.

  • Romeo Doubs (WR – GB): Christian Watson gets the hype, but Doubs has built chemistry with Love and gets red zone looks

  • Trey McBride (TE – ARI): With Kyler back at 100%, McBride could break into the top 5 TE tier.

  • J.J. McCarthy (QB – MIN): If he wins the job, he has top-10 upside in 2QB leagues with Jefferson and Addison downfield.

Where to Host Your Fantasy Draft

Looking to level up this year’s draft? Here are top Canadian bars that welcome fantasy football parties:

Toronto

  • The Ballroom – Private rooms, projectors, and food packages.

  • The Pint – Group booths, wing specials, and plug-ins for laptops.

Calgary

  • Hudsons Canada’s Pub – Draft beer, draft boards, and football-friendly space.

Vancouver

  • Malone’s Taphouse – Quiet booths upstairs perfect for focused GMs.

Montreal

  • Burgundy Lion – Combines pub tradition with strong Wi-Fi and whisky.

Draft Night Checklist

  • Confirm your league rules (Superflex? Half-PPR?)

  • Book your bar in advance (especially Thursday nights)

  • Bring a printed cheat sheet (Wi-Fi can betray you)

  • Have a “stay-sober GM” to track picks
