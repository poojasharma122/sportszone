Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 02, 2025

Like most websites, SportsZone uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience and analyze site traffic.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve functionality, remember user preferences, and gather analytics data.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function properly (e.g., navigation, security).

  • Performance Cookies: Used for tracking site usage via tools like Google Analytics.

3. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third parties (e.g., embedded maps, videos, or analytics tools). We do not control these cookies, and you should refer to the respective provider’s cookie policy.

5. Consent

By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. You will be prompted to accept or manage cookies upon your first visit.
