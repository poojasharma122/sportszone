Best Sports Bars British Columbia

Vancouver

These venues are BC’s go-to spots for game-day energy, big screens, and craft brews:

1. Shark Club Sports Bar & Grill

180 W Georgia St

Huge HD screens, stadium-level audio, top-tier atmosphere, ideal for NHL, NFL, UFC fans.

Check It Out

2. Score on Davie

1262 Davie St

West End staple since 2007 with karaoke, patios, strong local cult following.

Check It Out

3. The Pint Public House

455 Abbott St

Known for 40 wing sauces, dance floor, trivia nights, and a lively vibe.

Check It Out

4. Malone’s Taphouse

608 W Pender St

Craft beer on 38 taps, multiple sports on big screens, plus live music and trivia.

Check It Out

5. Red Card Sports Bar + Eatery

560 Smithe St

European-style sports bar with premium ambiance, top-rated by local polls.

Check It Out

6. BC Kitchen – Parq Vancouver

Stadium District

American comfort food, big screens, and excellent service

Check It Out

7. Sportsbar LIVE! at Rogers Arena

99 W Georgia St

Adjacent to the arena, perfect for pre- or post-game crowds.

Check It Out
